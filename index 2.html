Proven tips by category

Skincare
Highest ROI
✦

SPF Every Single Day

Sunscreen prevents premature aging, hyperpigmentation, and texture damage. SPF 30–50 daily is non-negotiable. It's the #1 anti-aging product in existence.

Free / Cheap
✦

Moisturize Consistently

Well-hydrated skin looks plumper, smoother, and healthier. Use a simple moisturizer morning and night — ceramide-based formulas work for nearly every skin type.

Easy
✦

Retinol / Retinoid at Night

Clinically proven to stimulate collagen, reduce fine lines, even skin tone, and improve texture over 12–16 weeks. Start with low-strength retinol 2x/week.

Invest
✦

Vitamin C Serum (AM)

Brightens skin tone, fades dark spots, and boosts SPF effectiveness when layered underneath. Apply after cleansing and before moisturizer each morning.

Invest
✦

Drink 2–3L of Water Daily

Hydration from within improves skin elasticity, reduces dullness, and minimizes the appearance of fine lines. One of the most underrated free habits.

Free
✦

Never Sleep with Makeup / Pollution

Double cleanse each night. Residual oxidative stress from pollution + makeup causes collagen breakdown and clogged pores over time.

Free

Body & Lifestyle
Long-Term Gains
✦

Reduce Body Fat

Losing fat reveals underlying bone structure. Cheekbones, jaw definition, and eye area all improve dramatically at lower body fat percentages. Aim for 10–15% BF.

Free
✦

Sleep 7–9 Hours Per Night

Poor sleep causes dark under-eye circles, puffiness, dull skin, and cortisol spikes that accelerate aging. Quality sleep is free anti-aging therapy.

Free
✦

Reduce Alcohol & Cut Smoking

Alcohol causes facial puffiness, broken capillaries, and skin dehydration. Smoking ages skin by 10–20 years. Eliminating these improves appearance dramatically within weeks.

Free
✦

Mew (Proper Tongue Posture)

Resting the tongue on the roof of the mouth applies gentle upward pressure on the palate over time. Most effective when started young but beneficial at any age.

Free
✦

Reduce Salt & Processed Food

High sodium causes water retention which leads to facial puffiness. Cutting processed food also reduces inflammation which shows on skin as redness and breakouts.

Free
✦

Train Neck & Jaw Muscles

Mastic gum chewing builds the masseter muscle for a more defined jawline. Neck training improves posture and creates a more proportionate facial/neck ratio.

Easy

Copper Peptide GHK-Cu

GHK-Cu (Glycyl-L-Histidyl-L-Lysine Copper) is one of the most researched peptides in skincare. It naturally occurs in human plasma but declines sharply with age. Topically applied, it penetrates the dermis to activate fibroblasts, remodel collagen, accelerate wound healing, and act as a potent antioxidant. Multiple clinical studies confirm visible skin-tightening and anti-aging effects with consistent use over 8–12 weeks.

Matrixyl 3000

One of the most studied anti-aging peptides. Signals fibroblasts to produce new collagen and hyaluronic acid. Clinically shown to reduce wrinkle depth by up to 33% after 2 months. Pairs well with retinol.

Argireline (Acetyl Hexapeptide-3)

Dubbed "topical Botox" — inhibits neurotransmitter release to reduce repetitive muscle contractions that cause expression lines. Most effective on forehead and eye area. Use in a serum, 2x daily.

Daily Protocol
Your optimized routine
Morning

AM Protocol

  • Splash face with cold water
  • Gentle cleanser if oily/combo skin
  • Vitamin C serum — wait 5 min
  • Matrixyl or Argireline serum
  • Moisturizer with hyaluronic acid
  • SPF 50 — last step, every day
  • Brush + floss, mouthwash
Evening

PM Protocol

  • Double cleanse — oil cleanser first
  • Niacinamide or AHA/BHA (3x/week)
  • Peptide night: GHK-Cu serum
  • Retinol night: 0.1–0.5% retinol
  • Rich moisturizer or night cream
  • Lip balm
  • Silk pillowcase, 7–9 hours sleep
Weekly

Weekly Habits

  • Exfoliate 1–2x (chemical)
  • GHK-Cu mask or boosted serum
  • Check brows, trim strays
  • Trim beard / body hair
  • 3–5x gym for body comp
  • Review diet — cut sodium
  • 1 rest day, elevated legs
